Concept Breakdown

"Explain [ topic ] the way you'd explain it to a topper in one page — core mechanism first, then the one exception examiners love to test."

Exam-Eve Recall

"Give me a rapid-fire recap of [ topic ] — only the facts, values, and terms that show up most often in past papers. No explanations, just the recall list."

Active Recall Drill

"Quiz me on [ topic ] one question at a time, examiner-style — don't reveal the next question until I answer, and increase difficulty as I get things right."
