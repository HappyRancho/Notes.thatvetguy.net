Pro Tip

Turn any recap sheet into a self-test

Don't just re-read the PDF. Upload it to an AI chat and have it quiz you back — you'll find the gaps a lot faster than by scanning the page one more time.

Copy this prompt

"I've attached my recap sheet. Quiz me on it one question at a time — mix objective (one-word/one-line) and short-answer style questions. Don't tell me if I'm right or wrong until I've answered, then correct me and flag anything I got wrong so we can circle back to it at the end."
